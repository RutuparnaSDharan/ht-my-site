Yum Yum Algorithm

The "Yum Yum Algorithm" revolutionizes your culinary experience by offering a user-centric platform to discover, create, and share recipes. Whether you're looking for a quick meal or a gourmet dish, this website allows you to find recipes by ingredients, cuisine types, and cooking time. It also enables users to share their creations and engage with a community of food enthusiasts.

Recipe Discovery & Sharing

Users can browse a vast collection of recipes and find inspiration for their next meal. The search algorithm allows for precise filtering based on various criteria, while the community feature enables users to share their own recipes and interact with others.

Interactive Cooking Guides

The platform provides interactive cooking guides and step-by-step instructions to help users through every stage of meal preparation. From planning to presentation, the guides ensure an enjoyable cooking experience.

Technology & Security

Utilizing technologies like PHP and MySQL for backend and integrating with APIs like MealDB, "Yum Yum Algorithm" ensures a secure and robust platform for recipe management. User authentication and data management are handled efficiently to provide a seamless and secure experience.
